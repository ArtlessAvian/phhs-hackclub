About Us

Who are we?

Hack Club is an after-school coding club dedicated to building a community for coders and encouraging them to create innovative projects to share with the world. When we say "hack", we mean using code to solve complex problems and make cool things, not breaking into other people's bank accounts.

Who can join?

Hack Club is open for anyone to join, whether you know how to code or not. We accept people of all genders, races, and skill level. Our goal here is to help people start on building projects using programming and web development skills, not to create an exclusive all-boys-club for STEM students.

What do we do?

Every Tuesday, students come into the club room and collaborate on group projects, and can have the possibility of presenting it during a meeting. We aim to structure our club meetings like mini-hackathons, meaning we will also provide concise workshops that help beginners familiarize themselves with coding concepts and logic.
