our mission

To bring students together through a mutual passion for coding and furthering programming literacy by community outreach and interactive workshops.

What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is an after-school coding club around the globe.

Here at Piedmont Hills, we dedicate every Tuesday teaching any curious students about programming and web development that are not taught in school.

Subjects include Java, HTML, CSS, xand JavaScript.

When we say "hack", we do not mean we sit in a dark room with ski masks on, installing viruses on school computers during club meetings. What we mean by "hacking" is using code to build cool things and solve complex problems.
