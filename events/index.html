We are proud to announce the first meeting of our rebranded coding club, Hack Club! Previously known as C0d3x, we still aim to do the same thing: get kids who aren't taking any computer science courses started on coding!

At our first meeting of 2017-2018, we'll be introducing what we'll be covering over the school year, as well as cool events we'll be attending! There also might be pizza. ;)

The meeting will be held in Mrs. Pereira's room in F28 after 7th period.
