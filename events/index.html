First Meeting on 9/12/17!

We are proud to announce the first meeting of our newly rebranded Hack Club! Our mission is still the same: to bring coding to those who are interested! Whether you're in AP Computer Science or not, we aim to help students learn more about the wonders of programming, this time with the help of Hack Club!

Hack Club provides an array (get it?) of helpful and quick workshops that help students learn how to create cool apps such as games, chat rooms, and websites using languages ranging from JavaScript to Ruby!

The meeting will be held in Mrs. Pereira's room in F-28.
