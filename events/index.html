First meeting on 9/12/17!

Hey there! We, formerly known as C0d3x, are proud to announce our first meeting as the newly rebranded Hack Club!

Our purpose is still the same: to ignite a passion for coding in students, and creating a community where coders can just hang out and share ideas. Here at Hack Club, we aim to teach those new to computer science the basics of web development and programming, but we help those taking AP Computer Science too! Don't worry, we have other activities for those already well versed in coding, which we'll be announcing during the meeting.

You can find us in Mrs. Pereira's room at F-28 (there will be snacks). Hope to see you soon!

PS: If you have not registered to be a part of Hack Club yet, simply go to our registration page and after submitting, you're in.
